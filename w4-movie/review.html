Star Wars Ep 3: Revenge of the Sith

ABOUT CAST REVIEW

Review of: Revenge of the Sith 2/10/2022By Stephen M

Star Wars Revenge of the Sithundoubtably is one of the strongest entires in the prequel trilogy. It delivers upon backstory of undenyably one of the most iconic movie villains of all time while providing fast-paced action packed scenes in which the original trilogy lacked in conjunction with all the other Star Wars movies I whole heartedly believe everyone should watch this movie.

Trivia Facts

  • George Lucas himself makes a cameo in this film as a background extra
  • The iconic ship "The millenium falcon" can be seen in the background shortly after the chancellor is rescued
  • George Lucas's son makes a brief cameo as a jedi youngling.
