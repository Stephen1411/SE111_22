About Revenge of the Sith

Three years into the clone wars, the republic is still in a major conflict with the seperatists. With Obi-Wan pursuing a new threat, Struggling with rules layed out by the jedi order and his immense fear of losing his wife Padme Anakin Skywalker is stuck between the jedi council and a plot to destroy the republic and rule the galaxy. This sci-fi/action film was produced by Lucasfilm in 2005. It has a run time of 2 hours and 20 minutes and is rated PG-13.
